Phases of best disease

The phases of Best disease:

  1. Back of the eye is normal, the eye movement is not normal
  2. Pre-vitelliform stage
  3. Vitelliform stage (looks like a sunny side up egg)
  4. Retina looks like scrabled egg
  5. Cyst forms
  6. "Pseudohypopyon" stage. Where cells collect together into a clump
  7. Atrophic stage
